## What is Audio Branding?

The strategic use of sound elements to express and reinforce a brand's identity. By crafting a distinctive audio logo, custom soundscapes, musical motifs and tailored effects, you create an emotional bridge between your audience and your brand - one that visuals alone can't achieve.
